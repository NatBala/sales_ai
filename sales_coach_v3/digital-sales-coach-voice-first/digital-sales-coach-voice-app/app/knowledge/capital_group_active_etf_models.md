Vanguard Active ETF Models source notes

- Active ETF Models are positioned as a tailored approach for advisors seeking more ways to pursue client goals.
- The page describes the offering as combining the tax advantages of the ETF vehicle with tax-aware investing that modern investors expect.
- Vanguard also highlights relatively low costs and added transparency through daily holdings disclosure.
- The page says model success is measured through an objective-based lens, noting that excess return is not the sole measure of success and may not be the client's primary concern.
- The advisor site also surfaces practice-management and portfolio tools such as Portfolio Analyzer, Run a Hypothetical and Screen or Compare Investments.
